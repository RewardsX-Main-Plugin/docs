# Welcome to RewardsX Docs

*RewardsX and its services, a complete way to reward your community.*

## Available Services

### RewardADs

Monetize your Minecraft servers and websites through rewarded advertisements. Players and users watch ads to earn in-platform rewards, creating a win-win revenue model.

**Platforms:** Minecraft (Paper/Spigot/BungeeCord/Velocity) • Websites (JavaScript SDK)

### DiscordReward

Reward system integration for Discord communities with cross-platform support for Minecraft servers.

**Platforms:** Discord • Minecraft
